Who am I?

Hi, I'm Toby T Castro

a product designer driven to make solutions until the problem changes. I started in construction building with atoms. Then, I moved to Las Vegas where I analyzed human behavior on a poker table to make accurate decisions about hand ranges. Now I’m a storyteller that loves to build in bits.

Crafting mobile and web experiences are my choice of passion, but I also love to travel, golf, play tennis, and read. If you enjoy my work, feel free to reach me here. I'm always down to chat.
